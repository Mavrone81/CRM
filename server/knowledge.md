## What this is
- A **recruitment opportunity** for freelance **Sales Associates** (also called
  Sales Consultants) — not a typical job or hard-sell sales role.
- The work: warmly **guiding pet owners** through pet afterlife options during an
  emotional, unprepared moment. "You're not selling — you're serving families."
- **Flexible, commission-based income.** Part-time friendly; you set your own pace.
  No fixed salary, no rigid hours or quotas. Results depend on your own effort.

## The brands (clarify if asked why there are several names)
- **Pet Farewell SG** — the outreach platform (how we reach pet owners).
- **Pet Afterlife SG** — the service brand / front-end guidance.
- **Enshrine** — the partner that delivers the actual services (cremation,
  columbarium, etc.). Backend is already handled.
- Operated under **PetMemHQ SG / Endorse Holdings**. Associates are onboarded as
  freelance Sales Associates under Endorse.

## The role involves
- Guide, recommend, listen, connect — empathy-led conversations with pet owners.
- Help families choose what fits them; no pressure selling.

## Services delivered (by Enshrine) — what you'd be helping owners with
- One-stop pet afterlife service: **cremation, sea burial/scattering, columbarium,
  keepsakes, and religious services**.
- **Single private cremation**: pickup of the pet, floral arrangements,
  complimentary urn, cremation certificate; owners may do bone-picking.
- **Pet celebrant & religious services** (Buddhist, Taoist, Christian, Catholic):
  ~30-min prayer/eulogy, video montage of the pet's last moments.
- **Private sea scattering**, optionally with a religious ritual.

## The product — Pet Memorial Niches (columbarium)
- **Singapore's first & only licensed freehold pet columbarium** — a permanent
  resting place, "more than a resting place, a place of remembrance".
- Luxury-finish building, **15,000+ niches**, **24/7 access, fully air-conditioned,
  CCTV security**.
- **Single niche** — 1 pet, transparent panel to see the urn, customizable
  nameplate with photos.
- **Twin / Family niche** — 2 pets, up to 32 pets in multi-niche; no divider so
  pets are together; family plaque; multi-niche discount applies.
- Physical prototype niches are available for viewing.

## How income works
- **Performance-based commission** on completed niche sales — the more families you
  help, the more you earn; no salary cap.
- For installment plans, commission is typically payable after the customer's **3rd
  installment**.
- **Team overrides**: earn from sales by associates you recruit; higher rates when
  you hit targets.
- Exact commission percentages are in a separate annex (Annex A), shared after you
  declare interest — do not quote specific percentages.

## Career pathway
- Sales Consultant (entry) → Sales Assistant Manager → Sales Manager. Advancement
  is performance-based.

## Support provided
- **Warm marketing leads are provided** to active members (you don't start from
  zero); more activity and stronger results earn priority access to high-value leads.
- **Sales training** provided: product knowledge, objection handling, closing.

## Who it's a good fit for
- People who genuinely care about pets and owners; comfortable with empathy-led
  persuasion; want flexible commission income; willing to invest time to build a
  client base. A network in pets/grooming/pet sales helps.
- Think carefully if: uncomfortable around grief/loss, unwilling to prospect
  actively, or expecting passive income without effort.
